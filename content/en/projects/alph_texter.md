## How it works
    
A matplotlib pyplot is embedded as a tkinter widget. It is animated and updates the bar graph 10 times a second. 

Includes functionality for A-Z, Z-A, A-Z (With Spaces), Z-A (With Spaces), A-Z (Shuffled)
