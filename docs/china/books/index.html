China Books

2022-01-25
1 min read

China books that I have read.

Politics

The following books analyze China’s politics, including foreign policy, domestic policy, history, governance, and culture.

Title (Author)Description
Haunted by Chaos
(Sulmaan Khan)		Decent summary of PRC and CPC history. Starts with Mao, transitions to Deng, ends with Xi.
The Party: The Secret World of China’s Communist Rulers
(Richard McGregor)		I purchased this book but have not read it yet.
The American Trap
(Frédéric Pierucci)		Became popular during Trump’s sanctioning of Huawei. Details how USFG uses the DoJ to influence foreign businesses.

Economics

The following books analyze China’s economic policies, initiatives, and realities.

Title (Author)Description
Up and Out of Poverty
(Xi Jinping)		Documentary of efforts to open up the economy of Fujian Province.
The China Questions
(Jennifer Rudolph, Michael Szonyi)		A good introduction into modern China for the uninitiated.
